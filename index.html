Our Model

We strive to create a thriving musical community by providing everything necessary for musicians to thrive, grow, and connect with other artists.

Educate

We provide lessons for a wide variety of instruments and vocal styles, for students of all ages.

Perform

Performance opportunities at Intermezzo are in abundance! Students may participate in recitals or create their own musical solo or collaborative events.

Collaborate

Not only do we strive to create a collaborative musical community within the studio, we connect musicians with opportunities to network with other local artists.
